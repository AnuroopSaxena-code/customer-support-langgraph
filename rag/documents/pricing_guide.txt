PRICING & SUBSCRIPTION GUIDE

Our software is offered under three main subscription tiers:

1. Starter Plan:
- Price: $19.99 per user / month (billed monthly) or $15.99 per user / month (billed annually).
- Ideal for small teams or individuals.
- Includes basic project management tools, 10 GB file storage, and email support.

2. Pro Plan:
- Price: $49.99 per user / month (billed monthly) or $39.99 per user / month (billed annually).
- Ideal for growing businesses.
- Includes advanced integrations, custom branding, 100 GB file storage, and priority email & chat support.

3. Enterprise Plan:
- Price: Custom pricing (contact Sales at sales@company.com).
- Tailored for large organizations.
- Includes dedicated account manager, unlimited file storage, custom SLA, and 24/7 phone support.

Free Trial:
We offer a 14-day free trial on the Starter and Pro plans. No credit card is required to sign up. After the trial ends, the account will be temporarily frozen until a subscription is activated.

Discounts:
Non-profits and educational institutions are eligible for a 20% discount on all plans. Please submit a support ticket under the Billing department to apply.
